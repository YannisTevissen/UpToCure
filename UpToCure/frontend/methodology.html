Our Methodology

AI-Assisted Research Report Generation

UpToCure's reports on rare disease research are generated using advanced language models fine-tuned for medical content. Our process involves:

  • Data Collection: Systematic review of peer-reviewed publications, clinical trial databases, and authoritative medical resources.
  • Content Generation: AI-powered synthesis of research findings with specialized prompting to ensure medical accuracy.
  • Expert Validation: Review process to check for hallucinations and factual errors, though this process is not exhaustive.
  • Citation Integration: All reports include references to original research sources.

Deep Research Agent Architecture

At the core of our methodology is a specialized AI research system built on the SmolAgents framework, which autonomously gathers and analyzes the latest scientific information on rare diseases. The system employs:

  • Web Search Capabilities: Our deep research agent navigates the web to find the most relevant and recent scientific publications, clinical trials, and research updates.
  • Document Analysis: The system can analyze PDFs, academic papers, and complex medical databases to extract pertinent information.
  • Multi-Agent Collaboration: A manager agent coordinates with specialized search agents to efficiently gather comprehensive information about specific diseases.
  • Medical Context Understanding: Fine-tuned language models help interpret complex medical terminology and research methodologies.

Conclusion

While we strive for accuracy, users should be aware that our AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies or outdated information. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions and refer to the original research sources cited in our reports for comprehensive information about rare disease treatments and research developments.
