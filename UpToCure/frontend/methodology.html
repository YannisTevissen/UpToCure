Our Methodology

AI-Assisted Research Report Generation

UpToCure's reports on rare disease research are generated using advanced language models fine-tuned for medical content. Our process involves:

  • Data Collection: Systematic review of peer-reviewed publications, clinical trial databases, and authoritative medical resources.
  • Content Generation: AI-powered synthesis of research findings with specialized prompting to ensure medical accuracy.
  • Expert Validation: Review process to check for hallucinations and factual errors, though this process is not exhaustive.
  • Citation Integration: All reports include references to original research sources.
